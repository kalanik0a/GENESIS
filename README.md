## What is GENESIS?

A unified program on the embodiment of artificial cognition and the grammar of its body. It composites two arguments:

1. **The Embodied Cognition Thesis** — The barrier separating a contemporary LLM from a genuine android is no longer physics or materials science. It is systems integration and developmental method. Both are tractable today.

2. **Synesthesia AI** — The synthesizer's control vocabulary (envelopes, filters, LFOs, routing matrices) is domain-agnostic. The same envelope that opens a filter can open a valve. Three problems — video-to-audio synthesis, affective sonification, and embodied actuation — are one problem expressed in three render targets.

The composite thesis: cognition is implementation-independent. A sufficiently capable reasoner lacks only the peripheral apparatus of a body and the developmental opportunity to inhabit it. Both are obtainable.
